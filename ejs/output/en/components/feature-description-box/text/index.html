The iPhone 13 CH is Apple's new flagship mobile phone that has entered the market with several new features and a dual camera. The iPhone 13 display is equipped with a Super Retina panel to provide very desirable images to the user. This display has a very high resolution; so much so that in its 6.1-inch size, it packs about 460 pixels per inch. It is possible to charge the battery of this phone wirelessly. Face detection using the front camera is another feature used in the new Apple iPhone. In terms of hardware, this phone uses the new A15 chip, which has 15 billion transistors and offers desirable heat control so that, in addition to normal tasks, it can support new virtual reality features that Apple is particularly focusing on these days. According to Apple itself, this phone is 50 times faster than its version 12. The processor has a new modular design that has significantly reduced battery consumption, giving you higher battery life. The display quality on the iPhone 13 is 120 Hz and you will witness high quality. Apple has included a GPU in this series of its iPhone phones that is 30 percent faster than its series 12 phones, and this promises that you can run games with heavier and larger graphics and maps. One of the features we see in the iPhone 13 is its cinematic ProRes video recording system, which can revolutionize mobile phone videography. Not active phones become active by inserting internal SIM cards, and unlike the active model, the product seal is not broken. Active Apple brand phones are also like this because due to limitations on some Apple products for Iran, they are officially activated and sealed with a single insertion of a SIM card in neighboring countries, and you should know that this process does not cause any problems regarding the product warranty. European, Japanese, and UAE part numbers are single SIM, and UAE-related part numbers do not support FaceTime either. With the JA (Japan) part number, even in silent mode, the photo shutter sound cannot be muted.
